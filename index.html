ABOUT OUR NGO

Crime Control & Social Development Organisation (CCSDO)
Towards a Safer, Empowered, and Sustainable Society

Crime Control & Social Development Organisation (CCSDO) is a committed non-governmental organisation (NGO) actively working in the domain of ESG – Environmental, Social, and Governance. Established by Ms. Kavita Rawat, CCSDO is driven by a strong mission to create positive, measurable change for society, and with society.

In just four years, we have taken significant strides in implementing impactful initiatives that focus on community upliftment, social justice, safety, environmental awareness, and good governance. Our work is grounded in collaboration, compassion, and long-term development goals.

Our vision is to build a society that is safe, inclusive, empowered, and sustainable. We believe in working hand-in-hand with communities to address critical challenges and co-create solutions that foster growth, dignity, and opportunity for all.

Impact Story

Transforming 150 Young Lives in Yamuna Khadar 🌟 In the heart of Yamuna Khadar, where access to quality education and opportunities is limited, our mission took root — to not only educate but also empower.

Empowering Yamuna Khadar Children

Over the past months, we have reached and impacted the lives of 150 students, providing them with basic education and introducing them to practical life skills. These children, many of whom had never stepped into a formal classroom, now confidently read, write, and express themselves with joy and curiosity.

Changing Lives Through Education

But our mission didn’t stop at books. Recognizing the need for holistic development, we also introduced skill-based learning — from creative arts and communication to computer literacy and basic life skills. These learnings have sparked confidence and ambition in every child, nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

What began as a small initiative is now a growing movement of change. The smiles, the spark in their eyes, and the eagerness to learn — that’s our biggest reward.

Together, we are not just educating children, we are empowering a generation.
