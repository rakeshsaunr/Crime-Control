Crime Control & Social Development Organisation (CCSDO)

CCSDO stands for Crime Control & Social Development Organisation. We are a non-profit working to reduce crime by improving society through education, awareness, health, skill development, and community programs.

Our NGO was founded on 15th October 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic. We began by supporting hospitals and communities through PPE kits and later expanded to long-term social development work.
