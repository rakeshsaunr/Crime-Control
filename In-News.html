Eye Camp for Children – Yamuna Khadar (at our Office)

Many of these children had never undergone an eye test before. Our aim was to ensure that poor vision does not become a barrier in their learning and growth. With the support of our medical partners, children were also provided with free spectacles and advised on proper eye care, helping them move towards a brighter and clearer future.

Eye Camp for Women – Kalyanpuri

A dedicated Eye Camp for Women was conducted in Kalyanpuri, focusing on the eye health of underprivileged women who often neglect their own health while caring for their families. The camp provided free check-ups, consultations, and spectacles to those in need. This small step brought not only medical assistance but also awareness about the importance of vision health in daily life, empowering women to take care of themselves with the same dedication they show towards their families.

Women’s Day Celebration – Marathon in Gurgaon

To celebrate the spirit of womanhood, we organized a special Women’s Day Marathon in Gurgaon, where over 150 women participated with great enthusiasm and energy. The event aimed to promote health, unity, and empowerment among women from all walks of life.

Commissioner of Police, Ms. Kavita Ji

We were honored by the presence of Commissioner of Police, Ms. Kavita Ji, who graced the occasion as the chief guest. Her encouraging words and active participation inspired many, especially the young women present. The marathon was not just a race, but a movement celebrating strength, resilience, and the unstoppable spirit of women.

Drawing Competition at Trilok Puri: 150 Young Artists, One Message.

In Trilok Puri, over 150 children came together to express their imagination and values through a vibrant drawing competition. 150 Young Artists, One Message

In Trilok Puri, over 150 children came together to express their imagination and values through a vibrant drawing competition. The theme focused on moral values and social awareness, encouraging children to reflect on topics like honesty, kindness, environment, and unity. Each stroke of color told a story of hope, dreams, and the change they wish to see in the world.

The event wasn't just about art

The event wasn't just about art — it was about giving children a platform to think, express, and grow. All participants received gifts and appreciation, inspiring them to continue exploring their creativity. It was a joyful celebration of talent, values, and the power of expression.

Empowering Women through Handmade Craft Stalls

Empowering Women through Handmade Craft Stalls in Corporate Spaces

As part of our women empowerment initiative, we organized stalls in corporate offices, where women entrepreneurs showcased their handmade products — from handcrafted accessories and home décor to eco-friendly items and ethnic wear. These stalls were more than just a marketplace; they were a platform to promote the skills, creativity, and hard work of women who are turning their talents into livelihoods.

Corporate employees and management

The initiative received overwhelming support from corporate employees and management, who appreciated the unique products and the powerful stories behind them. Through this effort, we aimed to build confidence, generate income, and create visibility for these talented women, encouraging them to grow as independent entrepreneurs.
