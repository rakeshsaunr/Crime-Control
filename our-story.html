Our Story

The journey of Crime Control & Social Development Organisation (CCSDO) began on 15th October 2020, during one of the most challenging times in recent history — the COVID-19 pandemic.

While the world struggled with uncertainty and fear, our team stepped forward to serve. We began with what we could do best: helping people directly on the ground. We distributed masks, created and delivered PPE kits for hospitals, and—most importantly—generated employment for many women by involving them in the production and distribution process.

This was not just relief work. It was the seed of a larger vision.

As the pandemic slowly faded, we asked ourselves a deeper question:

contact now
“What makes a society truly safe and secure?”

And the answer was clear — Social Development.

We started working with communities, helping them understand that crime is not just a legal issue, but a social one.

When people are aware, educated, empowered, and live in dignified environments, crime reduces naturally.

From that realization, we evolved our mission:
To control crime through social development, and to achieve that, we committed ourselves to work on ESG – Environment, Social, and Governance.

  • Through Environmental awareness, we promoted responsibility and respect for our surroundings.
  • Through Social initiatives, we educated and empowered women, youth, and children.
  • Through Governance awareness, we trained communities about their rights, duties, and workplace ethics like POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment).

Today, our journey continues with stronger partnerships, deeper impact, and an unwavering belief:
When society develops, crime disappears.
