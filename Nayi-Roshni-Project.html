Nayi Roshni - Empowering Underprivileged Children in Yamuna Khadar

The Crime Control and Social Development Organisation (CCSDO), in collaboration with Navv Khush Helping Foundation, launched the Nayi Roshni Project to empower 120 underprivileged children in Yamuna Khadar. The initiative provides holistic education, life skills training, and essential supplies like uniforms and stationery.

Additionally, pick-and-drop services ensure these children have safe and easy access to education. Launched on November 4, 2024, the project is building a brighter, more secure future for children through consistent support and development.

IMPACT

HOLISTIC CHILD DEVELOPMENT

Empowered 120 children with academic learning, life skills, and moral values—fostering better school attendance, confidence, and performance.

ESSENTIAL SUPPORT SERVICES

Provided uniforms, stationery, and transport facilities to ensure consistent access to education and promote social and emotional well-being.
